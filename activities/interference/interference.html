One thread through all three labs

Read the path difference

The simulations change representation, but the decision stays the same: compare Δ with the wavelength λ.

Δ = nλ In-phase sources: waves arrive aligned → constructive interference.
Δ = (n + ½)λ In-phase sources: crest meets trough → destructive interference.
