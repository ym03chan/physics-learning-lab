動量、碰撞與衝量 Momentum, Collisions & Impulse

依照概念發展次序，由動量的大小與方向開始，進入碰撞分類，再把衝量應用到撞擊力與安全設計。

Follow a concept-first path from momentum magnitude and direction to collision classification, impact force, and safety design.

The recommended sequence is 1 → 4, though each activity can also stand alone.

1 10–15 分鐘10–15 min

一維動量基礎

1D Momentum Foundations

質量、速度、方向與反彈 Mass, velocity, direction, and rebound

比較質量與速度的影響，利用正負號表示方向，並分析反彈前後的動量變化。

Compare the effects of mass and velocity, represent direction with signs, and analyse momentum change during a rebound.

核心問題Focus question 反彈前後速率相同，動量變化會是零嗎？ If the speed is unchanged after a rebound, is the momentum change zero?

2 15–25 分鐘15–25 min

碰撞分類任務

Collision Classification Missions

v–t 圖、總動量與總動能 v–t graphs, total momentum, and total kinetic energy

從速度—時間圖讀取數據，計算碰撞前後總量，再以證據辨別彈性、非彈性及完全非彈性碰撞。

Read velocities from a velocity–time graph, calculate totals before and after, and classify three collision types from evidence.

核心問題Focus question 動量守恆是否代表動能也一定守恆？ Does conservation of momentum guarantee conservation of kinetic energy?

3 10–15 分鐘10–15 min

撞擊力情境實驗

Impact Force in Context

硬地、軟墊與汽車緩衝區 Hard surfaces, cushions, and crumple zones

比較相同動量改變下的不同停止時間，連結軟墊、緩衝區與平均撞擊力。

Compare different stopping times for the same momentum change and connect cushioning and crumple zones to average impact force.

核心問題Focus question 兩個物體都停下，為何受到的平均力可以不同？ If both objects stop, why can their average forces be different?

4 15–20 分鐘15–20 min

衝量關係實驗室

Impulse Relationships Lab

控制變量與力—時間圖 Controlled comparisons and force–time graphs

分別固定 Δp、平均力或作用時間，觀察另一個量如何改變，並以圖像面積連結衝量。

Hold momentum change, average force, or interaction time constant and use graph area to connect the variables through impulse.

核心問題Focus question 「時間較長」何時令力變小，何時令動量改變變大？ When does a longer time reduce force, and when does it increase momentum change?
