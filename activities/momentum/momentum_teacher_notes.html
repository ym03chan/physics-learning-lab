動量、碰撞與衝量：模擬器教學指南

Momentum, Collisions & Impulse: Simulation Teaching Guide

四個模擬器由「動量是有方向的量」逐步發展至碰撞證據、撞擊力及衝量關係。重點不是讓學生自由試玩，而是透過預測、控制變量、記錄數據與以證據解釋，建立可遷移的概念。

The four simulations progress from signed momentum to collision evidence, impact force, and impulse relationships. The emphasis is not free play, but prediction, controlled comparison, data recording, and evidence-based explanation.

學習目標

Learning goals

完成活動後，學生應能以符號、計算及文字證據處理以下概念。

By the end of the sequence, students should be able to use signs, calculations, and written evidence to address the following ideas.

1｜動量的大小與方向1 | Magnitude and direction

使用 p = mv，按既定正方向為速度及動量加上正負號。

Use p = mv and assign signs to velocity and momentum from a stated positive direction.

2｜動量變化2 | Change in momentum

使用 Δp = pf − pi 解釋反彈時即使速率相同，Δp 仍不為零。

Use Δp = pf − pi to explain why equal rebound speeds do not imply zero momentum change.

3｜碰撞分類3 | Collision classification

以系統總動量、總動能及是否黏合，辨別三種碰撞。

Classify elastic, inelastic, and completely inelastic collisions using total momentum, total kinetic energy, and sticking.

4｜衝量與撞擊力4 | Impulse and impact force

使用 J = Δp = FavgΔt 比較相同 Δp、相同力或相同時間的情況。

Use J = Δp = FavgΔt to compare cases with equal Δp, equal force, or equal interaction time.

課前準備

Preparation

學生已有知識Prerequisite knowledge

質量與速度、動能公式、基本代數，以及「向右為正／向左為負」的符號約定。

Mass and velocity, the kinetic-energy formula, basic algebra, and the convention right = positive / left = negative.

物資Materials

每組一部電腦或平板、計算機、記錄紙或工作紙；全班示範時使用投影。

One computer or tablet per pair, calculator, recording sheet or worksheet, and projection for whole-class demonstration.

二人角色Pair roles

A 操作及讀圖；B 計算及檢查正負號。每完成一個任務交換角色。

A operates and reads graphs; B calculates and checks signs. Swap roles after each mission.

課堂規則Working rule

先寫預測才播放；先記錄數值才按下一個任務；解釋必須引用至少一組數據。

Write a prediction before playing; record values before moving on; every explanation must cite at least one numerical comparison.

教師提示：Teacher cue: 每次比較只改變一個主要變量。若同時改變質量、速度與時間，學生容易把相關性誤當因果。 Change one main variable at a time. Simultaneous changes to mass, velocity, and time make it easy for students to confuse correlation with causation.

建議教學流程

Suggested teaching routes

精簡 45 分鐘路線

Focused 45-minute route

0–5
引入：反彈是否代表 Δp = 0？ Hook: Does a rebound give Δp = 0?

展示相同入射及反彈速率，要求學生先投票，不立即公布答案。

Show equal incoming and rebound speeds. Collect a vote before revealing any calculation.

5–15
模擬器 1：方向與反彈 Simulation 1: Direction and rebound

集中使用「方向很重要」及「反彈與動量變化」兩部分。

Focus on “Direction Matters” and “Bounce & Change.”

15–32
模擬器 2：三關碰撞任務 Simulation 2: Three collision missions

二人合作完成至少兩個碰撞類型，並以 p 及 KE 證據分類。

Pairs complete at least two collision types and classify using momentum and kinetic-energy evidence.

32–41
模擬器 3：安全情境遷移 Simulation 3: Transfer to safety contexts

比較硬地／軟墊或堅硬車身／緩衝區，完成句式解釋。

Compare hard/soft surfaces or rigid/crumple-zone cars and complete the explanation frame.

41–45
離堂票 Exit ticket

一題正負號、一題碰撞分類、一題 F–Δt 因果解釋。

One sign question, one collision-classification question, and one force–time causal explanation.

完整 70–80 分鐘路線

Full 70–80-minute route

完成上述流程後，加入模擬器 4 的三種控制變量比較，並要求學生畫出或解讀力—時間圖的面積。亦可分成兩課：第一課使用模擬器 1–2；第二課先重溫 Δp，再使用模擬器 3–4。

After the focused route, add the three controlled comparisons in Simulation 4 and ask students to sketch or interpret force–time graph area. Alternatively, split the sequence into two lessons: Simulations 1–2 first, then review Δp before Simulations 3–4.

四個模擬器的教學用途

Teaching purpose of each simulation

1

一維動量模擬器

1D Momentum Simulator

質量與速度 → 方向 → 反彈與 Δp Mass and velocity → direction → rebound and Δp
開啟Open

教師動作

Teacher moves

  • 要求學生每次只改變質量或速度其中一項。
  • 在改變速度正負號前，先問箭咀會否反向。
  • 反彈例子必須逐行寫出 pi、pf、Δp。
  • Require students to change mass or velocity, not both.
  • Before changing the sign of velocity, ask whether the arrow will reverse.
  • For rebound, write pi, pf, and Δp on separate lines.

可見學習證據

Observable evidence

  • 學生能說明「速率相同」不等於「動量相同」。
  • 能把向左速度寫成負值。
  • 能解釋反彈時 Δp 的量值可大於 |pi|。
  • Students explain why equal speed does not guarantee equal momentum.
  • They record leftward velocity as negative.
  • They explain why rebound |Δp| can exceed |pi|.
2

碰撞分類器：三關任務

Collision Sorter: Three Missions

漸退支架、v–t 圖及證據分類 Faded scaffolding, v–t graphs, and evidence-based classification
開啟Open

教師動作

Teacher moves

  • 先確認正方向，再從圖中讀取四個速度。
  • 任務一示範如何比較「系統總量」，不可只看單一小車。
  • 任務二交換角色；任務三要求學生先在紙上計算。
  • State the positive direction before reading four velocities from the graph.
  • Model comparison of system totals, not one cart in isolation.
  • Swap roles in Mission 2; calculate on paper before Mission 3 submission.

分類判據

Classification evidence

  • 彈性：總 p 守恆；總 KE 守恆。
  • 非彈性：總 p 守恆；總 KE 減少；物體分開。
  • 完全非彈性：總 p 守恆；總 KE 減少；末速度相同。
  • Elastic: total p conserved; total KE conserved.
  • Inelastic: total p conserved; total KE decreases; objects separate.
  • Completely inelastic: total p conserved; total KE decreases; final velocities match.
3

撞擊力測試實驗室

Impact Force Lab

雞蛋、軟墊與汽車緩衝區 Egg drops, cushions, and car crumple zones
開啟Open

教師動作

Teacher moves

  • 先問兩個情況的 Δp 是否相同，再比較時間。
  • 避免只說「軟墊吸收力」；要求使用 Favg = Δp/Δt。
  • 讓學生完成頁內三格因果句式。
  • First establish whether Δp is the same, then compare stopping time.
  • Avoid “the cushion absorbs force”; require Favg = Δp/Δt.
  • Have students complete the three-part causal sentence frame.

目標解釋

Target explanation

兩者的質量、初速度及末速度相同，因此 Δp 相同。B 的停止時間較長，所以 B 的平均力較小。

Both cases have the same mass, initial velocity, and final velocity, so Δp is the same. Case B has a longer stopping time, so its average force is smaller.

4

衝量實驗室

Impulse Lab

三種受控比較與力—時間圖 Three controlled comparisons and force–time graphs
開啟Open

三種比較

Three comparisons

  • 相同 Δp：時間越長，平均力越小。
  • 相同平均力：時間越長，Δp 越大。
  • 相同時間：Δp 越大，平均力越大。
  • Same Δp: longer time gives smaller average force.
  • Same average force: longer time gives larger Δp.
  • Same time: larger Δp gives larger average force.

教師動作

Teacher moves

  • 每次先圈出「保持不變」的量。
  • 要求學生以圖形的闊度、高度及面積解釋。
  • 教師模式可延伸至峰值力及不同脈衝形狀。
  • Circle the controlled quantity before each comparison.
  • Require explanations using graph width, height, and area.
  • Teacher mode can extend to peak force and pulse shape.

常見迷思與回應

Common misconceptions and responses

「較重的物體一定有較大動量。」 “The heavier object always has more momentum.”

以「重但靜止」對比「輕但快速」，要求同時引用 m 和 v。

Compare a heavy stationary object with a light fast object; require reference to both m and v.

「相同速率即相同動量。」 “Equal speed means equal momentum.”

把 +4 m/s 與 −4 m/s 並列，要求畫出相反方向箭咀。

Place +4 m/s beside −4 m/s and require opposite momentum arrows.

「反彈前後速率相同，所以 Δp = 0。」 “Equal rebound speed means Δp = 0.”

堅持使用 pf − pi，不要只比較大小。

Insist on pf − pi; do not compare magnitudes alone.

「動量守恆表示每架車的動量不變。」 “Momentum conservation means each cart keeps its momentum.”

把兩車視為系統，比較碰撞前後的總和，而非個別值。

Treat both carts as one system and compare totals, not individual values.

「非彈性碰撞中動量不守恆。」 “Momentum is not conserved in an inelastic collision.”

明確分開兩個判據：孤立系統總 p 守恆；總 KE 可能減少。

Separate the criteria: total p is conserved in an isolated system, while total KE may decrease.

「作用時間越長，力一定越小。」 “A longer interaction always means a smaller force.”

先問哪一個量保持不變。只有相同 Δp 時，時間較長才代表平均力較小。

Ask what is held constant. Longer time implies smaller average force only when Δp is fixed.

高效課堂提問

High-leverage classroom questions

提問應迫使學生辨認系統、方向、控制變量及證據，而非只讀出螢幕上的答案。

Questions should force students to identify the system, direction, controlled quantity, and evidence rather than merely read the screen.

你選擇哪一方向為正？哪一個速度必須寫成負值？
Which direction is positive, and which velocity must be negative?
你正在比較單一物體，還是由兩架車組成的系統？
Are you analysing one object or the two-cart system?
哪一個量在兩個情況中保持不變？你如何知道？
Which quantity is held constant in the two cases, and how do you know?
哪一組數據支持你的碰撞分類？只說「看起來反彈」是否足夠？
Which numerical evidence supports your classification? Is “it looks bouncy” sufficient?
力—時間圖變闊、變高或面積變大，分別代表甚麼？
What does a wider, taller, or larger-area force–time graph represent?

形成性評估與離堂票

Formative assessment and exit ticket

巡視時檢查During circulation

正方向是否寫出；向左速度是否為負；KE 是否使用 v²；比較的是總量還是單一物體；解釋有否引用數據。

Check stated positive direction, negative leftward velocities, use of v² in KE, system totals rather than one object, and numerical evidence in explanations.

口頭回應句式Oral response frame

「因為 ______ 保持相同，而 ______ 增加／減少，所以根據 ______，______ 會……」

“Because ______ is held constant while ______ increases/decreases, the equation ______ shows that ______ will…”

三題離堂票 Three-question exit ticket
  1. 一個 2.0 kg 物體由 +3.0 m/s 反彈至 −2.0 m/s。計算 Δp。
  2. 某孤立碰撞的總動量守恆，而總動能減少，兩物體碰撞後分開。判斷碰撞類型並寫出兩項證據。
  3. 兩個相同物體由相同速度停下。B 的停止時間是 A 的三倍。比較兩者平均力並解釋。
  1. A 2.0 kg object rebounds from +3.0 m/s to −2.0 m/s. Calculate Δp.
  2. In an isolated collision, total momentum is conserved, total kinetic energy decreases, and the objects separate. Classify it and give two pieces of evidence.
  3. Two identical objects stop from the same speed. B takes three times as long to stop as A. Compare their average forces and explain.
參考答案：Answer guide: 1. −10 kg·m/s；2. 非彈性碰撞；3. Δp 相同，B 的時間為三倍，因此 B 的平均力為 A 的三分之一。 1. −10 kg·m/s; 2. inelastic collision; 3. same Δp, so three times the stopping time gives one-third the average force.
