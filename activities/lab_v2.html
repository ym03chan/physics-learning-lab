Two ways to explore time

Both activities are bilingual and browser-ready. Choose the scale-matching game for breadth, or the pair investigation for measurement method and evidence.

For a 10–15 minute Exploratorium-style lesson, start with Version 2.
Version 1 · Game
10–15 minSolo / pair

Time Scale Match

Match Hong Kong, everyday and scientific events to durations spanning femtoseconds to billions of years. An optional clock run adds retrieval practice.

Best forOrder of magnitude, scientific notation, unit conversion and scale sense.

Time Lab: Pair Investigation

Collect human click data, compare timing one and five pendulum swings, swap roles, and use the evidence to design a better measurement method.

Best forMeasurement uncertainty, repeated measurements, fair methods and evidence-based reasoning.
