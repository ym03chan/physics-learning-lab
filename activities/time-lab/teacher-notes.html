Teacher notes

Two activities, two learning purposes

Use Version 1 to build time-scale sense. Use Version 2 to investigate how measurement method affects the quality of evidence.

At a glance

Version 1: Time Scale Match

Focus: order of magnitude, approximate duration, scientific notation and unit conversion.

Grouping: individual or pairs.

Time: 10–15 minutes.

Version 2: Pair Investigation

Focus: human timing variation, repeated-event timing, uncertainty and method choice.

Grouping: pairs with A/B roles.

Time: 10–15 minutes.

Version 1 lesson notes

Learning intentions

  • Estimate and compare durations across very different time scales.
  • Connect familiar units to seconds and scientific notation.
  • Use order of magnitude before attempting exact conversion.

60-second launch

“The values are approximate. Your job is not to memorise every number. First decide the power of ten, then use the familiar unit to check your choice. In pairs, one person proposes a match and the other must give a reason before placing it.”

Suggested flow

TimeAction
0–2 minExplain approximate values and ask students to identify the fastest and slowest events.
2–9 minPairs complete the matching board. Ask them to verbalise one conversion.
9–12 minOptional clock run for retrieval practice.
12–15 minDebrief: identify one surprising pair and explain the scale difference.

Hints and answers

Allow the built-in hint limit to preserve discussion. The answer notes unlock after completion. Emphasise that several contextual durations are approximate, so the target is scale sense rather than false precision.

Key conceptual check: A value such as 180 s and 120 s belongs to the same order of magnitude even though the events are not the same duration.

Version 2 lesson notes

Learning intentions

  • Observe that human responses are delayed and vary.
  • Explain why timing several cycles and dividing reduces the effect of endpoint variation per cycle.
  • Choose an automatic method when a short, one-off event requires higher precision.

60-second launch

“Partner A touches the controls first; Partner B watches, counts and challenges the claim. You will swap. Do not race another pair—your own variation is the evidence.”

Suggested flow

TimeAction
0–1 minAssign A/B roles and read the big question.
1–4 minComplete three flash trials and interpret the click range.
4–9 minSwap roles; time one and five pendulum swings.
9–12 minComplete the two method-design cases.
12–15 minDebrief using the pair’s evidence label.

What to watch for

  • Students start and stop at the same point in the pendulum cycle.
  • They distinguish a lucky accurate result from a method with lower expected uncertainty.
  • They do not claim that repeated measurement removes every error.
Important limitation: the click range is a simple classroom proxy for human timing variation, not a clinical reaction-time measurement. Browser animation also has finite timing accuracy. These limitations are useful discussion points.

Expected transfer answers

For a repeating wheel: time 10 turns, repeat and divide by 10. For a one-off close race: use a light gate or high-frame-rate video. Correct reasoning: timing more cycles makes endpoint variation a smaller fraction of the total interval.

Practical preparation

  • Use one laptop or iPad per pair. Version 2 needs both students to see and reach the same screen.
  • Test full-screen mode and browser zoom before class.
  • No network connection is required once the files are loaded.
  • For students sensitive to motion, the activity respects the device’s reduced-motion setting.

活動概覽

版本 1：時間尺度配對

重點：數量級、近似時距、科學記數法和單位換算。

分組：個人或二人。

時間：10–15 分鐘。

版本 2：二人探究

重點：人為計時變化、重複事件、不確定度和方法選擇。

分組：設有 A／B 角色的二人組。

時間：10–15 分鐘。

版本 1 教學筆記

學習目標

  • 估算和比較跨度很大的事件時距。
  • 把常用單位連繫至秒和科學記數法。
  • 先判斷數量級，再進行精確換算。

60 秒開場

「活動中的數值是近似值，不用背下每個數字。先判斷十的次方，再用常用單位核對。二人合作時，一人提出配對，另一人必須先說出理由才可放置。」

建議流程

時間活動
0–2 分鐘解釋近似值，找出最快和最慢事件。
2–9 分鐘完成配對，要求學生口述至少一次換算。
9–12 分鐘可選限時挑戰，進行提取練習。
12–15 分鐘找出一組令人驚訝的事件，解釋尺度差異。
概念檢查：180 s 和 120 s 雖然不是相同時距，但屬於同一數量級。

版本 2 教學筆記

學習目標

  • 觀察人為反應有延遲並會變化。
  • 解釋為何量度多個週期再相除，可減少每個週期所受的端點變化影響。
  • 在短暫的一次性事件需要較高精密度時選擇自動量度方法。

60 秒開場

「A 同學先操作；B 同學觀察、數數和質疑結論，中途會交換角色。不要跟其他組比快，你們自己的變化就是證據。」

建議流程

時間活動
0–1 分鐘分配 A／B 角色，閱讀核心問題。
1–4 分鐘完成三次閃光測試並解讀點擊極差。
4–9 分鐘交換角色；量度一個和五個擺動。
9–12 分鐘完成兩個量度方法設計情境。
12–15 分鐘利用小組的證據標籤總結。

觀察重點

  • 學生在擺動週期的同一位置開始和停止。
  • 學生能分辨「一次碰巧準確」和「預期不確定度較小的方法」。
  • 學生不應聲稱重複量度能消除所有誤差。
重要限制：點擊極差只是課堂上估計人為計時變化的簡化指標，並不是臨床反應時間測試；瀏覽器動畫亦有有限的計時準確度。

遷移題答案

重複轉動的輪子：量度 10 圈、重複，再除以 10。一次性的接近衝線：使用光閘或高幀率影片。理由：量度更多週期，可令端點變化只佔總時距較小部分。

課前準備

  • 每組使用一部手提電腦或 iPad；版本 2 需要兩位學生同時看見和操作畫面。
  • 課前測試全螢幕模式和瀏覽器縮放。
  • 檔案載入後毋須網絡連線。
  • 活動會尊重裝置的「減少動態效果」設定。
